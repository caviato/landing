How it works

Request an item

Place a request on our platform. Gives us as much information about the item as you can (eg. where to buy, price, link to website, ...)

Wait for an offer

We will send your request to our network of travellers, and notify you when there is someone offering their services.

Make payment and receive your good

Once you accept an offer that you like, make a payment to Caviato. Only when you confirm that you have received the goods, will we release the fund to the seller.

Sign up with us as a traveler

We will give you access to our list of requests from buyers. You can pick and choose request that align with your travel destination and timeline.

Make an offer

You can decide how much money you would want to claim as reward for completing a request. We will send your offer to the buyers and notify you when they accept it.

Complete the request and receive the reward

After completing the request, just send out a request for fund release to us and you will receive the reward immediately in your bank account.
